Semantic  
Pertaining to meaning as produced by the interaction between signal and a semantic field.


Semantic Field  
A structured space in which signal resolves into meaning.  
Its structure reflects how past meaning determines future meaning.


Signal  
Any phenomenon, such as communication, which changes a semantic field.


Meaning  
The effect signal produces on a semantic field.


Constraint  
Anything which decreases the entropy of a semantic field.


Resolution  
The process by which signal produces meaning within a semantic field.


Semantic Entropy  
The degree of uncertainty within a semantic field regarding how signal will resolve into meaning.
